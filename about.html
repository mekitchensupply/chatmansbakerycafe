About Us
With many endearing years of experience, we boast a highly skilled team of baking professionals.  It is our pleasure to assist with bakery orders of every capacity. 

See Debra Chatman, Owner of "Chatman's D'Vine Bakery & Cafe" on Washington DC's Nine News Now Kitchen.

In her 20 year Law career Debra never imagined she'd be preforming her passion baking up a storm.
She has now made a dramatic career change from Law to an entrepreneurial quest to deliver the highest quality of fresh baked pies and cakes. 

She is now the owner of Chatman's D'Vine Bakery and Cafe in the Washington Convention Center.

Debra joined Howard Bernstein for a lesson on how to bake pound cakes.
